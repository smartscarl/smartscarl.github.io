关于编辑 · 郑文 (Zheng Wen)

郑文（Zheng Wen）负责本站打字 / 输入法工具的编辑与维护。本站打字速度测试完全在浏览器中运行，不上传任何内容。基准与方法综合自公开资料（维基百科：Words per minute、Typing），数据为参考估算，非一手实测。

Zheng Wen (郑文) edits and maintains the typing and input-method tools published here. The typing speed test on this site runs entirely in the browser and uploads nothing. Benchmarks and methodology are compiled from public references (Wikipedia: Words per minute; Typing); figures are reference estimates, not first-party measurements.
